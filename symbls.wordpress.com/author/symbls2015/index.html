Friday, 6th November 2015 – Divinity School, St. John's College, Cambridge

Author: symbls2015

Overview

May 24, 2015May 24, 2015 symbls2015

The Symposium for Biological and Life Science Students (SymBLS) is an annual event for postgraduate students from all life science institutes and departments at the University of Cambridge. The symposium is a platform for students to discuss current issues in life science as well as to present their own research to peers from across the University.

This year, the 10th edition of SymBLS will take place on November 6th, 2015 at Divinity School – St. John’s College, Cambridge.

Keynote speakers to be announced in the coming weeks.

We are looking forward to seeing you in November.

SymBLS 2015 Organising Committee
