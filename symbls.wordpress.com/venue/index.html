Friday, 6th November 2015 – Divinity School, St. John's College, Cambridge

Venue

SymBLS will be held at  St John’s College, Cambridge. The whole day will take place within the Divinity School, a newly renovated conference venue within the college. Talks will be held in the impressive 190-seat lecture theatre, and the sponsor booths, poster session, and lunch/coffee breaks will be held in adjoining rooms on site. The Divinity School will be signposted from the main college entrance on the day.

More details about how to get to college can be found here.
